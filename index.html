About the House

A private harbour operation, run like a hotel

Luxury Yachts Dubai operates a small, closely maintained fleet from Dubai Harbour — the closest berth to Palm Jumeirah, the Marina skyline and the open Gulf. Every charter is captained by a licensed crew and prepared to the same standard, whether it's a two-hour sunset run or a full-day charter for twelve.

No call centres, no third-party brokers. You speak directly with the team that boards you.
