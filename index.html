Before You Board

Frequently
asked.

Anything not covered here, our concierge team will answer directly — by call or WhatsApp.

How far in advance should I book?

+

Standard charters can often be confirmed same-day. For super yachts, weekends, or groups above 20 guests, we recommend booking 3–5 days ahead to secure your preferred vessel.

Is a boating license required?

+

No. Every charter includes a licensed captain and crew. You're a guest on board, not the operator — no license or experience is needed.

Are crew, fuel and catering included?

+

Crew and fuel are included in every quoted rate. Catering can be added when you book, from light refreshments to a full plated menu.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure. Changes within 48 hours are handled case-by-case by your concierge coordinator.

How do I pay for a charter?

+

We accept card, bank transfer and cash on request. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of your charter.

What's the largest group you can host?

+

Our largest party yachts comfortably host up to 40 guests. For larger events, we can arrange multiple vessels sailing together.
